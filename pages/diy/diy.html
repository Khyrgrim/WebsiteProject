PROJECT 01: 3D Printing



I use a DiscoEasy200 from Dagoma (Fr) 3D printer to create some useful (or not) things like game board pieces, furniture improvements. I make my 3D design with Fusion 360 (Autocad) software and sometime Blender for special tasks. You can find some of my design shared on Thingiverse which a website where numerous 3D model can be found for various application. In my designs description you can find more details on the 3D printing parameters I used (Temp,Filling% ...).

PROJECT 02: Paper Modeling



I use Pepakura to make the paper pattern, and then I assemble the pieces and solidify them with epoxy resin (and glass fibre sometimes). It's really easy to print, mount and paint with acrylic painting after.

PROJECT 03: Geometric Origami



I discover this kind of origami with the youtuber "Micmath" with this video. I really like geometric forms and the great stellated dodecahedron he realized in its video is one of my favourite. The small stellated dodecahedron is also a real piece of art (It's the geometric form used for the stars in Super Mario Galaxy ^^).
