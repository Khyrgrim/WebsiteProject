Carots and red lentils salad

Healthy, fresh and sweet salad. Raw carots are really healhty, vitamines and fiber rich. Red lentils bring energy and are protein rich. The combination with olive oil and balzamic vinegar goes very well.


Ingredients

- Carots (60 g)
- Red Lentils (60 g)

Optionnal:
- Baby Spinach (50 g)
- Glaze or Balsamic and olive oil
- Vegetable stock cube
- Cumin

Instructions

- Grat the carots.
- Cook the lentils separetly (10 minutes in boiling water with stock cubes and/or cumin).
- Cool the lentils with cold water and let drain.
- Slice the baby spinach leaves into strips .
- Put everything together and mix.
- Add glaze or mix with balsamic vinegar, olive oil and mustard.

Notes

For balsamic sauce, put 2 tbsp of blasamic vinegar, 2 tbsp of olive oil and a tsp of mustard. Baby spinach leaves add a little bit of color, taste and texture.
