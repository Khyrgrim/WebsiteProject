Pancakes

Really simple, and delicious pancakes. I learned this receipe in Japan, during my trip in the Chichibu mountains at Chris Page place and it was a revelation.


Ingredients

- Wheat flour (500 g)
- Backing powder (1 dose)
- Apple juice (1/2 L)
- Sunflower oil (1 tbsp)

Optionnal :
- Alcool (rhum or Grand Marnier)
- All you can put on pancakes:
(Chocolate, mapple sirup, banana, sugar, jam)

Instructions

- Mix the flour and the backing powder.
- Add apple juice until consistancy.
(The more thick is the paste, the more thick the pancakes will be and the longer they will need to cook)
- Add the alcool.
- Take a fring pan and wipe it with kitchen paper diped in sunflower oil.
- Pre-heat the fring pan (medium fire).
- Poor a kitchen string of paste in the pan and wait 2-3 minutes before to turn the pancake upside down.
- Alternate sides until cooked.
- Wipe with the kitchen paper between each pancake.

Notes

Depending on the amount of apple juice you use, you can obtain some thick pancakes or thin crepes. You can put some banana pieces in the paste in the pan to cook it in the pancakes ... delicious ^^.
