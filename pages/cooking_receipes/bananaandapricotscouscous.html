KikiCouscous

This is a sweet and spicy version of the traditionnal "bride couscous".


Ingredients

- Banana (1)
- Dry apricots (100 g)
- Raisins (100 g)
- Red curry paste (1 tbsp)
- Vegetable oil (1 tbsp)
- Coconut milk(100 ml)
- Peanut butter (1 tbsp)
- Couscous (120 g)

Instructions

- Chop the banana and apricots in 1 cm pieces.
- In a frying pan, heat some vegetable oil and put the curry paste in it.
- When the red curry sizzle let 1 minute more, then add the coconut milk and the fruits and let it cook 5 minutes .
- In a tuperwear put the same volume of couscous and boiling water the close it and let it like this 5 minutes.
- Put everything together and serve.
