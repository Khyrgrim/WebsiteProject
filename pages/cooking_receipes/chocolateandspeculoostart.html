Chocolate and speculoss tart

Deliciously unhealthy chocolate tart initially realized by Lucie, a cousin of mine. The Lu's poo tart is its original name.


Ingredients

- Speculoos (400 g)
- Margarine (5 tbsp)
- Dark chocolate for dessert (200 g)
- Oil : coco, hazelnut or walnut (90 g)
- Icing sugar (90 g)
- Non-dairy cream : coco or soya (10 cl)

Optionnal :
- Vanilla sugar (1 sachet)
- Rhum or Grand Marnier (1 tbsp)
- Grinded almond (50 g)
- Fruits: Pear or apple or rasberry

Instructions

- For the base:
- Finely grind the speculoos.
- Add the margarine and mix to obtian an homogeneous paste.
- Pack the paste in the mould and make a ridge.
- Cook in the oven, 25 minutes at 190 C.
- Let cool the paste.

- For the ganache:
- Melt the chocolate.
- Add oil and mix.
- Add sugar and vanilla sugar and mix.
- Add non-dairy cream and mix.
- Add ground almond and the alcool and mix.

- Dressing:
- Put the ganache on the paste.
- Put the fruits on the top.
- Put in the fridge few hours.

Notes

Ground almond are very good with the pear.
