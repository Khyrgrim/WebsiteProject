Butternut and Pineapple Tacos

I am not found of pineapple on pizza ... so I was sceptical at first ... but gosh, it send my taste bud in heaven. This receipe came from the "Tasty" website.


Ingredients

- Butternut squash (1)
- Pineapple (500g)
- Extravirgin olive oil (4 tbsp)

Marinade:
- Orange juice (3 tbsp)
- Apple cider vinegar (1 tbsp)
- Paprika (1.5 tsp)
- Chili powder (1.5 tsp)
- Cayenne (1/2 tsp)
- Dried thym (1.5 tsp)
- Ground coriander (1.5 tsp)
- Ginger (4 cm piece)
- Garlic (2 cloves)
- Koster salt (1 tsp)
- Black peper (1 tsp)

Tacos Toping:
- Corn tortilla (12)
- Avocado (1)
- Onion (1/2)
- Lime (1)
- Fresh coriander (1/2)

Instructions

The marinade:
- Put all the marinade ingredients and 2 tbsp of olive oil in a large bowl.

Butternut squash:
- Pre-heat the oven at 180 C.
- Cut the butternut squash in 1 cm cubes.
- Put the cubes in the marinade bowl and coat it, then it there for 10 minutes.
- Put the cubes on a baking tray and roast it in the oven 45 minutes.

For the pineapple:
- Cut the pineapple in 1 cm cubes.
- Put the cubes in the remaining marinade bowl and coat it, then it there for 5 minutes.
- Put the cubes on a baking tray and roast it in the oven 30 minutes.
- 5 minutes before the end, put back the roast with 2 tbsp of olive oil.

For serving:
- Cut the onions in little dice.
- Cut the avocado in little dice.
- Pluck the leaves from the coriander.
- Heat the tortillas (Oven or microwave).
- Assemble tacos by putting everything together in the tortillas depending on your taste.
- Serve with a lime slice.
