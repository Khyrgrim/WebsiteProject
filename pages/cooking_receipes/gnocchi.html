Gnocchi

Gnocchi are so easy and fun to make. So tasty with just olive oil but you can add any sauce you want.


Ingredients

- Potato or sweet potato (500 g)
- White flour (250 g)
- Salt (1 tsp)

Instructions

Making the base:
- Put the potatoes 10 minutes in the microwave oven (or 45 min at 220 C in the oven).
- Let it cool 15 minutes,then peel it and squash it.
- Mix little by little the flour and the salt with the potato to form an homogeneous dough.
- Divide the dough and roll it into sausage shapes then cut it and roll it on an indented surface (tip of a fork for example) to form the gnocchi.

Cooking the gnocchi:
- Put water to boil (with a little bit of salt or vegetable stock).
- Put the gnocchi in the boiling water.
- When they begin to float, let cook 30 secondes more, then remove from the water.
- In a frying pan, heat some vegetable oil and slightly fry the gnocchi.
- Serve with anything you want (from simple olive oil and fresh tomatoes to more elaborate sauce).

Notes

You can replace 250 g of potato by pumpkin and/or buternut squach for a change of flavor (cook it the same way).
