Hummus

Really simple, and delicious hummus.


Ingredients

- Canned chick peas (200 g)
- Tahin (sesame mush) (50 g)
- Olive oil (1 tbsp)
- Lemon juice (1 tbsp)
- Garlic (1 clove)
- Harissa (1/2 tsp)
- Water

Optionnal :
- Cumin (1.5 tsp)
- Paprika (1.5 tsp)
- Curry (1.5 tsp)

Instructions

- Grind everyting in a mixer.
- Add water and olive until you reach desired consistancy.
- Add optionnal spices depending on your tastes.
